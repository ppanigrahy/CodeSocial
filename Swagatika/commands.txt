***********Unix Commands***********************
Pwd - present Working dir.
Ls - list of files & directories.
Ls -lart - list of all files, hidden files etc.
Cd  - Change dir.
Cd .. - parent dir.
Mkdir - Make dir.
rm - Remove files.
rm -rf- remove directory recursively & by force.
touch <fileName>- create and update the file.
Cp path/ path2/.- Copy
vi/vim <File name> :q-close
		   :wq-Save &Close
		   :w-Save.
